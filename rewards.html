How it works

1. Sale routes to pool

25% of every Bepe Love NFT sale lands in the pool wallet automatically via SplitXCH.

2. Epoch closes

Every 768 blocks (~4 hours at POC settings), the epoch closes. The Worker snapshots NFT ownership at the last block.

3. Holders claim

Connect, sign a one-time challenge, and the Worker pays you everything you're owed across every unclaimed epoch in one transaction.
